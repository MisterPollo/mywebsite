Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MisterGorr's Discord Bots ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our Discord bots (APE-BOT, FORM-BOT, and ANIME-BOT). This policy applies to all users of our bots.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary for the operation of our bots:

  • Discord User IDs: We store Discord user IDs to facilitate transactions and manage user balances.
  • Transaction Data: We record blockchain transaction data related to tipping activities.
  • Command Usage: We may log command usage for debugging and improvement purposes.

We do not collect or store personal information beyond what is provided through Discord's API and what is necessary for our bots to function.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our Discord bots
  • Process and record blockchain transactions
  • Improve and optimize our bots
  • Troubleshoot issues and provide support
  • Prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your information may only be disclosed in the following situations:

  • Blockchain Transactions: Transaction data is recorded on public blockchains as necessary for the functionality of the bots.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Service Providers: We may engage trusted third parties to assist us in operating our bots, who will be obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your information
  • Objection to or restriction of processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the information provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
